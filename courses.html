Our Courses :

At The Intersectional Business Academy, our courses are designed for leaders, professionals, and entrepreneurs who want to grow with confidence while staying true to their values. Each program combines practical tools with emotional intelligence to help you thrive in every area of life and business.

Designed for You :

Whether you’re a busy executive, a young professional, or a community leader, these courses provide strategies and insights tailored for real-world success.

Women in Wisdom: Oh!daciously Ourselves

A transformative program for women ready to embrace their authentic power. Through storytelling, strategy, and community support, this course empowers women to lead boldly, strengthen confidence, and create lasting impact in both personal and professional spaces.

Cool, Calm and Collected: Trust in Yourself

Learn practical tools for resilience, emotional regulation, and confident decision-making. This course equips leaders and professionals with strategies to stay grounded under pressure, trust their instincts, and communicate with clarity in every setting.

The Gentleman’s Guide to Being an Ally: Building Inclusive Communities

A leadership course for men seeking to become active allies in business and community spaces. Explore practical frameworks for inclusive leadership, learn how to challenge bias, and build strong, respectful partnerships that uplift everyone.

Flexible Generations: Making Friends with Artificial Intelligence

This course bridges technology and human skills, helping participants understand AI’s role in modern business and life. Learn how to leverage AI tools responsibly, enhance productivity, and navigate the evolving workplace with confidence and adaptability.

Life Mastery for Young Adults

Designed for emerging leaders, this course equips young adults with essential life and career skills—financial literacy, communication, self-confidence, and resilience—so they can navigate adulthood with clarity, purpose, and strength.
